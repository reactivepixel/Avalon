What is Avalon?

Avalon is where artists of all kinds promote their creative work easily and effectively with other artists. We believe that you should be able to easily find the work of other like minded artists easily. If you doubt our service, please try and explore for content in Avalon and see how easy it is to find art.

Even services as disparate as YouTube and SoundCloud work together in unison to provide the best experience. Don't fear mixing your video content with audio content.
